The Titans: Bitcoin vs. Ethereum

Core Metrics Comparison

Ethereum's Proof-of-Stake model allows for significantly faster block processing than Bitcoin's Proof-of-Work.

Primary Purpose & Philosophy

While both are cryptocurrencies, their fundamental goals differ. Bitcoin aims to be digital money, while Ethereum is a platform for building decentralized applications.
